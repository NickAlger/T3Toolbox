t3tools.probing.compute_etas
============================

.. py:function:: t3tools.probing.compute_etas(outer_tt_cores: Sequence[t3tools.common.NDArray], mus, nus, xnp=np) -> Sequence[t3tools.common.NDArray]

   Compute downward edge variables associated with edges between Tucker cores and adjacent TT-cores.
   Used for probing a Tucker tensor train.

   See Section 5.2, particularly Figure 9 in:
       Alger, N., Christierson, B., Chen, P., & Ghattas, O. (2026).
       "Tucker Tensor Train Taylor Series."
       arXiv preprint arXiv:2603.21141.
       `https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21141 <https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21141>`_

   :param outer_tt_cores: Outer TT-cores for Tucker tensor train.
                          len=d. elm_shape=(ri,ni,r(i+1))
   :type outer_tt_cores: typ.Sequence[NDArray]
   :param mus: leftward edge variables mu. len=d+1. elm_shape=(num_probes,ri)
   :type mus: typ.Sequence[NDArray]
   :param nus: rightward edge variables mu. len=d+1. elm_shape=(num_probes,ri)
   :type nus: typ.Sequence[NDArray]
   :param xnp: Linear algebra backend. Default: np (numpy)

   :returns: downward edge variables eta. len=d+1, elm_shape=(num_probes,ni)
   :rtype: typ.Tuple[NDArray,...]

   .. seealso:: :py:obj:`probe_t3`, :py:obj:`compute_xis`, :py:obj:`compute_xis`, :py:obj:`compute_nus`, :py:obj:`assemble_probes`
