t3toolbox.probing.assemble_tt_variations
========================================

.. py:function:: t3toolbox.probing.assemble_tt_variations(weighted_sigma_tildes: t3toolbox.common.typ.Sequence[t3toolbox.common.NDArray], weighted_tau_tildes: t3toolbox.common.typ.Sequence[t3toolbox.common.NDArray], weighted_deta_tildes, weighted_xis: t3toolbox.common.typ.Sequence[t3toolbox.common.NDArray], weighted_mus, weighted_nus, sum_over_probes: bool = False, map=common.ragged_map, xnp=np) -> t3toolbox.common.typ.Tuple[t3toolbox.common.NDArray, Ellipsis]
