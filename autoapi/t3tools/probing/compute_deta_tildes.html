t3tools.probing.compute_deta_tildes¶

t3tools.probing.compute_deta_tildes(ztildes: Sequence[NDArray], basis_cores: Sequence[NDArray], use_jax: bool = False) → Tuple[NDArray, Ellipsis]¶

Adjoint-var-upward edge variables deta_tilde. Used for computing transpose of mapping from a Tucker tensor train tangent vector to its actions.

See Section 5.2.4, particularly Formula (43), in:

Alger, N., Christierson, B., Chen, P., & Ghattas, O. (2026). “Tucker Tensor Train Taylor Series.” arXiv preprint arXiv:2603.21141. https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21141
